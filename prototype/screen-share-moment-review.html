Screen share moment review

Decide when a shared screen should lead the layout, stay beside the speaker, or be skipped — so viewers can follow the conversation across a full-length episode.

Layout choices

Each screen moment can use one of three layouts. The choice applies to this moment only — other moments keep their own layout.

  • Featured — shared screen fills the frame; speaker is a small inset
  • Side by side — screen and speaker share the frame equally
  • Speaker only — hide the screen and keep the speaker full-frame
